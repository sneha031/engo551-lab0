Art
Art is one of the ways I destress. When life gets busy or stressful, creating something with my hands helps me slow down and breathe. I love the feeling of starting with a simple idea and turning it into something real. For me, art is not about feeling better, expressing my mood, exploring new art styles, enjoying the process, and creating something that I can say I made. Most of the time I'm engaging with the left side of my brain (due to school), and doing any form of art ensures that I use the right side of my brain and keep my creativity flowing.
