Art
Art is one of the ways I feel the most like myself. When life gets busy or stressful, creating something with my hands helps me slow down and breathe. I love the feeling of starting with a simple idea and turning it into something real. For me, art is not about being perfect. It is about expressing my mood, exploring new styles, and enjoying the process. I also like that creativity shows up in many forms, from painting to crochet to small craft projects.
