Space
Since I was young I have been facinated by Space. It has always captivated me and given me the reason to do well in school, and eventually pick my major as well. I love how Space is so vast, to the point where it is mid blowing, and it makes us realize how small we are compared to the universe. I also love how every single thing connects to it, whether it be having the ability to communicate with others (using satellite dishes) or the very atoms that make up our bodies.

Why I love space

I love space because it feels endless and as if no matter how much you learn, there's always more to see. It forces me to think bigger, motivates me to keep learning, and oddly enough makes me feel very connected to everything spiritually. I think Space is one of the biggest examples, of humanity coming together from all over the world to tackle a subject in science where no one has the complete knowledge, and unites us in a way that almost nothing can.

My fav things about space

  • Galaxies and how huge they are
  • How Earth looks from space
  • Black Holes, and how mind bending they are in nature
  • Discovering life and planets like ours
  • Potentially travelling to and from outer space (commercial useage)

I chose Geomatics Engineering to pursue my love for space and eventually be helping in creating technologies and analyzing data that would expand our knowledge in this subject greatly!
